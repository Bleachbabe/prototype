Info

Discover a carefully picked selection of electronic live acts, art performances as well as DJ sets and visual arts interventions spread over four rooms. More than 30 artists from the international, national and local scene will take over the entire complex of Gessnerallee over the weekend.

Since 2016, Backslash Festival has been presenting works that move between and beyond performing arts, club culture and visual arts. The festival is organised by the non-profit association Tokyo Data Collective, in co-production with the Theater Gessnerallee.

Awareness

At Backslash Festival, we have a ZERO tolerance policy towards discrimination, harassment, and abuse in any form. A dedicated Awareness team will be present at all times and a safer space for retreat will also be available.

Accessibility

Strobe lighting will be used now and then in the stages X and Y. There won't be any strobe lighting in the stages W and Z. The entire festival has been designed so that all areas are accessible for people with limited mobility.
