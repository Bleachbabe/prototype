FUMU

FUMU makes music that feels like memory — distant, half-formed, but impossible to shake. Her productions sit at the edge of ambient and club, dissolving form just enough to keep the floor guessing. Live, she layers found sounds and synthesised textures into something both fragile and devastating. Not a DJ. Not a producer. Something else entirely.
